Juan Morales

Juan Morales is a PostDoc researcher at CeSViMa, (Centro de Supercomputación y Visualización de Madrid), in the UPM (Universidad Poltéctina de Madrid).

All of his research is related and supported by the Cajal Blue Brain Project

Research

I am interested in: Visual Analytics, Human Computer Interaction, Data Analysis, Image Analysis.

All my research has been driven by user's needs practicing User Centered Design, so the outcomes have been software tools that fulfills the requirements of the users (usually the neuroscience community).

I have predilection for interdisciplinary groups. The communication is harder for sure, but the joy of resolving complex problems in different ways is unbeatable.
